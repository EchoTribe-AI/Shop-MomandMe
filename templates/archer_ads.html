Straight to Ads
Skip organic. Build the post and go directly into a paid Meta campaign. Best for time-sensitive deals or products with strong Archer commission rates.
Traffic destination
📄
Landing page
Ad traffic goes to a Mommy & Me shoppable page. Captures fbclid, fires Facebook Pixel events, then routes to Amazon. Best for data collection and retargeting.
⚡
Direct to Amazon
Ad traffic goes straight to the Amazon product page. Fewer clicks in the funnel. Less data. Test both to see which converts better for your audience.
